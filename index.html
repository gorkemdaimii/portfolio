I'm shooting a film today. But I'm actually archiving tomorrow.

I want to create work that, when we look back one day, will make people ask, “Who felt this first?”

I'm Daimii. I make commercials, shoot short films, and experiment with new visual languages using artificial intelligence. But my real focus isn't technology — it's people.

When I founded Daimi Company, I didn't envision an agency. I envisioned a future workshop. A place where feeling, artificial intelligence, and cinema sit at the same table.

Untamed, Mirror, Naked… These aren't a portfolio. They're trailers for a world to come.

For me, the camera isn't a machine. It's a message from within time.

If one day you look at my work and say, “This kid sensed the future,” — that's exactly why I'm here.

Ben daimii. Reklam filmi yapıyorum, kısa filmler çekiyorum, yapay zekâyla yeni görsel diller deniyorum. Ama asıl meselem teknoloji değil — insan.

Daimi Company’yi kurarken bir ajans hayal etmedim. Bir gelecek atölyesi hayal ettim. Hissin, yapay zekânın ve sinemanın aynı masaya oturduğu bir yer.
